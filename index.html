About Sattva Vasanta Skye

Sattva Vasanta Skye is a premium residential community by the esteemed Sattva Group, located in the fast-growing region of Devanahalli, North Bangalore. Spread across 16 acres, the project offers a thoughtfully planned mix of 1,077 homes ranging from smart studios to spacious 4 BHK apartments. Just minutes from Kempegowda International Airport, the location ensures excellent connectivity to key infrastructure, including business parks, schools, hospitals, and major highways.


Designed for modern living, Vasanta Skye combines elegant design with over 50 lifestyle amenities, such as a 1-acre clubhouse, rooftop swimming pool, sports courts, landscaped gardens, and dedicated leisure zones. With a focus on sustainability, the project features rainwater-fed water bodies, energy-efficient systems, and abundant open green spaces. Developed by the trusted Sattva Group, this landmark project is a perfect choice for both homebuyers and investors seeking value, comfort, and a future-ready lifestyle in one of Bangalore’s most promising growth corridors.

FAQs

Is Sattva Vasanta Skye suitable for investment or self-use?
Yes, the project is ideal for both. Its strategic location near the airport and upcoming tech parks makes it a great investment opportunity, while the premium amenities, spacious layouts, and green spaces make it perfect for self-living.
Does the project offer eco-friendly or sustainable features?
Yes. Sattva Vasanta Skye integrates sustainable design elements, such as:
-> Rainwater-fed central water body
-> Energy-efficient lighting
-> Ample open green areas
-> Cross-ventilation in apartment design
-> These features align with eco-conscious living.
Are there any hospitals, schools, or IT parks nearby?
Absolutely. The project is close to:
-> Columbia Asia Hospital, Aster CMI, and Manipal Hospital
-> Schools like Delhi Public School, Canadian International School, and VIBGYOR
-> Tech parks in Devanahalli Aerospace SEZ and Hardware Park
All these ensure comfort and connectivity for working professionals and families.
What is the construction status of Sattva Vasanta Skye?
The project was launched in May 2025, and construction is ongoing. The possession date is set for May 2031, giving ample time for buyers to plan phased payments or investments.
What amenities and community features does the project offer?
Residents can enjoy over 50 amenities, including: A 1‑acre clubhouse with multiple halls, rooftop pool, and al fresco dining Gym, swimming pool, children's play area, sports courts, jogging track, landscaped gardens, event lawn, meditation decks 24/7 security, double‑glazed windows for noise control, rainwater‑fed central water body for sustainability
